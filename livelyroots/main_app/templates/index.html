LivelyRoots

Real Families. Real Connections.

We have all experienced the frustration of other social media platforms. The drama, the lack of respect and kindness, and the realization that your "friends" aren't really your friends.

LivelyRoots is a platform for real families to make real connections with each other. If you haven't received an invite from another user, sign up by creating a new family. When you're finished, you'll be able to set up a user profile and invite your family to join you on LivelyRoots.

We're here to support family connections and bring honesty and transparancy back to social media. If you have any concerns or questions, please view our FAQs or contact us. We're here for you.

Features

child_friendly

LivelyRoots is safe for your kids because your "friends" are family.

security

There's no need to adjust privacy settings because everything you share can only be views by members of your family.

comment

Share only the things that matter to you, and see the same from your family.
